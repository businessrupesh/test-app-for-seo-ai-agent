Help

What is TestCart?

TestCart is a fictional e-commerce store used as an SEO testing fixture. None of the products are real and none of the orders go anywhere.

How do I return items?

Items can be returned within 30 days of delivery. Email support and we will send you a prepaid label. Refunds typically post within five business days.

What is your shipping policy?

We ship to most countries. Standard delivery is three to five business days; expedited is one to two. Free standard shipping on orders over $50.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes. At checkout you can choose gift wrapping for $5 per item. We use recycled paper and reusable ribbon.

How do I track my order?

After your order ships you will receive a tracking link by email. You can also see status in your account under Order History.

Can I change or cancel an order after placing it?

Yes, as long as it has not yet shipped. Contact support as soon as possible and we will do our best to make the change.

Do you ship internationally?

We ship to over forty countries. Duties and taxes are the responsibility of the buyer; rates are calculated at checkout.

How do I contact support?

You can email us, use the contact form, or chat with us in the bottom-right corner of any page during business hours.
